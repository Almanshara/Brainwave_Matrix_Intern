Our Mission

This application is designed to help students manage their tasks and take notes efficiently. Whether you need to track your assignments, homework, or personal tasks, this app provides a simple, user-friendly interface to keep you organized.

Features

  • Task management with a To-Do List
  • Note-taking functionality for students
  • Simple, intuitive, and responsive design

How to Use

Navigate through the app using the menu at the top. You can add tasks in the To-Do List page, and store and view your notes in the Student Notes page. It's easy to use, and we hope it makes your student life more organized!
